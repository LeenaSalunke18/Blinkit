Features

Light and sound notifications

Stay notified with Light and Sound about new Steem Blockchain actions such as new Steem account Upvotes, Followers, and Post from your favorite Steem users, bots and communities.

Supported Devices

Wide variety of devices can be used to blink: USB sticks, Philips hue lamps, Sonoff smart Wifi switches, Arduino's, Webcams, and Logitech keyboards, mice and headsets

Open source development

Improving the Blinkit project is rewarded and partly moderated by Utopian-IO. Are you interested to join Blinkit and get rewarded with Steem cryptocurrency? Get in contact on Discord for more details.

Steem Blockchain

Steem is a blockchain-based rewards platform for publishers to monetize content and grow community. use Blinkit to get notifications from it's users and communities, stay notified and anticipate on the actions the moment they happen.

Supported Devices

These devices can be used with Blinkit, to blink there lights and leds and do other device specific things. The "Read on Steemit" button will open the related posts on Steemit.com and will show more information related to the device features and implementation.

USB Flash Drives

Any USB flash drive that have a status LED, independent of brand or model can be used, Most preferable will be a USB stick with a bright LED and a small form factor.

Read on Steemit →

Keyboards

The Logitech brand is probably the most famous, input device manufacturer in the world, there keyboards and mice with RGB leds can be blinked with Blinkit.

Read on Steemit →

Smart Home Device

Inexpensive Sonoff Wifi switches can be used to switch on and off devices with a power plug such as regular lamps on newly detected Steem Actions.
